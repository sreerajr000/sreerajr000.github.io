Daily Blogs #1

This is weird. I am on a train metaphorically and literally. The fact that I don't know whether I am moving forward or backward or even standing still and just floating around in space is terrifying. I should enjoy it. But I am in an oddly blissful state. Going home is as always . A calling from an eternal prison, often an enjoyable one. But for some reason , "Come running home to you... Come running home... To you..." is what I am thinking. Am I seriously overestimating my mental capacity. Or am I being silly. To my defense I've never been silly. I've always tried to see the clearer picture in often foggier circumstances. I sympathize silliness, ignore. My pain is my satisfaction. And now I've inflicted that pain on to someone else too. And my pain now is... our pain.

"Sometimes it's only madness that makes us what we are" - Batman
