Daily Blogs #3

This is easy. Being me, ever so silent. State of sensory deprivation. In thoughts, ignoring reality, deprived of multiple senses, I am proceeding on to nothing. From emptiness to an urge of doing something happened in a moment. A moment's excitation and I am on track to do something. That's not natural to me. Then why am I doing. Because for the first time in a while someone actually listens. Multitudes of passive listeners and ignorant ones and jockers, finally someone to make it what it is, a conversation. And here I am, sitting in the exact same spot and yet again, I think the kingfisher wanted to be referenced again, flew pass me. The ode of this summer was you. The assonance you made with me was more than captivating enough for me to cage you in a fantasy land. My reality is my fantasy. And my fantasy is my pain. And now I've inflicted that pain on to someone else too. And my pain now is ... Our pain.
